About

A Designer Who Cares About the Player

My core passion has always been narrative, from my work on a Visual Novel QA, to VR game writing, to the quest and dialogue systems that shaped successful Roblox worlds and mobile games. I believe stories make systems feel meaningful.

Beyond writing, I bring a cross-disciplinary perspective: I've wired UX prototypes in Figma, sat in engineering standups, managed influencer campaigns, run live ops pipelines and covered for producers. I know what a game needs to actually ship, and I know how to make the team around me better.

I've worked with some of the most recognisable IPs in the world, including Mattel, Nickelodeon and global music acts, and I understand how to balance creative vision with stakeholder requirements.
